Research

I work in the area of language technology.

Recurring key aspects of my research are:

  • Methodology and fundamentals: Development of fundamental linguistic resources such as a part-of-speech tagset for Albanian.

Language technology

I have a strong interest in natural language processing (NLP). So far, I have been active in the following areas: tokenization, part-of-speech tagging, unsupervised dependency parsing, semantic similarity, implicit emotion recognition and sentiment analysis.
