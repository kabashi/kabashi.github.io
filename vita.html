Education and work

since 2020 Part of the teaching staff of European Master in Lexicography – EMLex in the advanced module Computational Lexicography. (International Elite Program).
since 2004 Various contracts as Researcher and Lecturer at Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich, Albanology.
since 2003 Researcher at the Chair for Corpus and Computational Linguistics, Friedrich-Alexander University of Erlangen-Nuremberg (FAU), Germany.
2020 Visiting lecturer at the University of Santiago de Compostela (UCS), Spain, Computational Lexicography (EMLex).
2018–2021 Co-working on the DFG-Projekt “Digitales philologisch-etymologisches Wörterbuch des Altalbanischen (15.-18. Jh.)” at the Ludwig Maximilian University of Munich, Albanology.
2018–2020 Researcher at the Fraunhofer IIS, Erlangen, Germany, Natural Language Processing.
2011–2014 Doktor der Ingenieurwissenschaften, (Dr.-Ing.), in Computer Science, Artificial Intelligence / Digital Humanities, Friedrich-Alexander University of Erlangen-Nuremberg, Germany.
Thesis: Automatische Verarbeitung der Morphologie des Albanischen (i.e. Automatic Processing of the Morphology of Albanian).
2009–2011 Visiting lecturer at the University of Tirana, Albania, Computational Linguistics.
2007-2011 Project Natural Language Production, Computational Linguistics, Friedrich-Alexander University of Erlangen-Nuremberg.
2004–2006 European Masters of Language and Speech, Friedrich-Alexander University of Erlangen-Nuremberg, Germany, The International Speech Association, & The European Chapter of the Association for Computational Linguistics.
1996…2003 Magister Artium, (M.A.), in Computational Linguistics (Linguistische Informatik), German Linguistics and Computer Science, Friedrich-Alexander University of Erlangen-Nuremberg.
